Creator Migration & Onboarding

Eliminate switching costs by automating content migration from existing platforms

Back to Overview

Current State

Problem: Creators face high switching costs when joining new platforms due to content recreation needs

Strategic Advantage

Eliminate switching costs through automated content migration and intelligent onboarding

Migration Strategy

  • OnlyFans Integration: API access (with permission) to extract previous posts and schedule on Instafans
  • Escortfans Migration: Automated cross-platform content sync and creator communication
  • Social Media Import: Instagram, TikTok content adaptation for creator profiles
  • AI-Powered Scheduling: Intelligent content distribution over time based on engagement patterns

Onboarding Optimization

  • Guided Setup: AI-powered profile optimization based on creator niche and audience
  • Content Strategy AI: Personalized recommendations for content types and posting schedules
  • Revenue Projections: AI-driven earnings estimates based on content type and audience size
  • Cross-Platform Promotion: Automated social media integration with AI-generated teasers

STRATEGIC INSIGHT

Content migration eliminates the primary psychological barrier to platform switching while providing immediate value

EXECUTION PRIORITY

  • Timeline: Months 2-5 (after payout infrastructure foundation)
  • Investment: Medium (development of migration tools and AI optimization)
  • Risk: Low (proven demand for content migration solutions)
