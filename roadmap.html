Implementation Roadmap

6-12 month strategic execution plan with measurable success metrics

Back to Overview

Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)

Month 1
LATAM Payout Infrastructure
  • Complete Zazoo integration for seamless creator payouts
  • Establish strategic banking partnerships in key LATAM markets
  • Launch beta testing with 50 creators
Month 2
Creator Migration Tools
  • Develop automated content migration from OnlyFans, Fansly
  • Create seamless onboarding flow for new creators
  • Launch referral program for existing creators
Month 3
Platform Optimization
  • Implement AI-powered content recommendations
  • Launch mobile app for creators and fans
  • Establish customer support in Spanish and Portuguese

Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-6)

Month 4
Referral Ecosystem Launch
  • Launch gamified referral system for creators and fans
  • Integrate social media sharing and viral mechanics
  • Partner with micro-influencers for platform promotion
Month 5
AI Landing Page Automation
  • Deploy AI-powered landing page optimization
  • Launch multi-market testing (Mexico, Brazil, Argentina)
  • Implement dynamic pricing based on market conditions
Month 6
Cross-Platform Integration
  • Launch E-I to Instafans user migration campaign
  • Integrate Escortfans creator base
  • Establish unified analytics dashboard

Phase 3: Scale (Months 7-12)

Months 7-9
Market Expansion
  • Expand to additional LATAM markets (Colombia, Chile, Peru)
  • Launch European market entry (Spain, Italy)
  • Develop creator education and support programs
Months 10-12
Platform Leadership
  • Achieve market leadership position in key LATAM markets
  • Launch advanced creator tools and analytics
  • Establish strategic partnerships with major brands

Risk Mitigation & Contingency Plans

Technical Risks

  • Backup payment processing solutions ready for deployment
  • Scalable infrastructure with auto-scaling capabilities
  • Comprehensive security audits and compliance checks

Market Risks

  • Diversified market entry strategy to reduce dependency on single markets
  • Flexible pricing models to adapt to competitive pressures
  • Strong creator support programs to maintain platform loyalty

Regulatory Risks

  • Legal compliance framework for each target market
  • Proactive engagement with local regulatory bodies
  • Adaptive content moderation policies
