CRITICAL PRIORITY

LATAM Payout Infrastructure

Solve the biggest barrier to creator adoption through Zazoo integration and strategic banking partnerships

Back to Overview

The Problem

Payout infrastructure is the primary barrier preventing creator adoption across all LATAM markets

Current Gaps

  • No reliable local banking partnerships in priority countries
  • Zazoo digital bank lacks clear direction and positioning
  • Creators forced to use suboptimal international transfer methods
  • Competitor platforms also struggling with same issue

CRITICAL PATH

Payout infrastructure is on critical path for platform launch. Delay in solving this blocks entire strategy.

Critical Resource Requirements

Current Gap: Zazoo strategic direction undefined, no dedicated banking partnership lead

CEO DECISION REQUIRED

  1. Approve Zazoo repositioning as creator-focused digital bank
  2. Authorize immediate hire of Banking Partnership Lead for LATAM markets
  3. Define interim crypto payout strategy while banking partnerships develop

Priority 1: Zazoo Positioning as Creator Bank (IMMEDIATE DECISION)

  • Role: Position Zazoo as the leading digital bank for creators in LATAM and Europe
  • Scope: Creator-specific features, multi-currency support, local payout integration
  • Approach: Creator-first branding and feature development
  • Impact: Differentiates entire ecosystem and solves industry-wide problem

Priority 2: LATAM Banking Partnership Lead (IMMEDIATE HIRE)

  • Role: Secure local banking partnerships in priority LATAM countries
  • Scope: Mexico, Colombia, Argentina, Chile, Peru partnership development
  • Approach: Focus on fintech partnerships and crypto solutions as interim measures
  • Impact: Enables platform launch with competitive payout advantages

Resource Impact on Timeline

  • Without banking solutions: Launch delayed 6-12 months, creator adoption limited
  • With Zazoo positioning: Immediate competitive advantage and ecosystem integration
  • With LATAM partnerships: 3-4 month launch timeline achievable

Payout Stack Strategy

Tier 1: Zazoo Creator Bank (Strategic Foundation)

  • Creator-focused digital banking with LATAM and European support
  • Multi-currency accounts with local payout integration
  • Creator-specific features: tax tools, earnings analytics, financial planning
  • Cross-platform integration with E-I and Escortfans ecosystem

Tier 2: Local Banking Partnerships (Immediate Priority)

  • Mexico: Partnership with fintech platforms for peso payouts
  • Colombia: Local bank integration for peso transfers
  • Argentina: Crypto-friendly solutions due to currency restrictions
  • Chile/Peru: Regional banking network partnerships

Tier 3: Crypto & Alternative Solutions (Launch Enabler)

  • Stablecoin payouts for immediate launch capability
  • Partnership with crypto exchanges in target markets
  • Creator education on crypto-to-fiat conversion
  • Integration with existing crypto payout platforms

Implementation Components

Solution: Multi-tiered payout infrastructure with creator-focused digital banking (requires immediate strategic decisions)

  • Zazoo integration with creator-specific features and multi-currency support
  • Priority country banking partnerships: Mexico, Colombia, Argentina, Chile, Peru
  • Interim crypto payout solutions for immediate launch capability
  • Creator education on payout options and financial management tools
  • AI-powered payout optimization based on creator location and preferences

CEO INPUT NEEDED

Zazoo strategic positioning timeline and budget allocation for banking partnership development

EXECUTION PRIORITY

  • Timeline: Months 1-4 (critical for platform launch)
  • Investment: High (foundational requirement for market entry)
  • Risk: High (platform cannot succeed without solving this)
